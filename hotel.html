Kerala Hotels & Resorts FAQs

Everything you need to know about booking hotels and resorts in Kerala

What types of hotels are available in Kerala?
Kerala offers a wide range of accommodations including luxury resorts, beachfront hotels, budget stays, heritage homes, and premium houseboats in Alleppey and Kumarakom.
Which are the best locations to book hotels in Kerala?
Popular hotel destinations include Munnar for hill resorts, Alleppey for houseboats, Kovalam and Varkala for beach resorts, and Thekkady for nature stays.
Do Kerala resorts offer family-friendly facilities?
Yes, most Kerala resorts provide family-friendly amenities such as swimming pools, spacious rooms, kids' activities, and guided sightseeing options.
Are houseboat stays included in hotel bookings?
Houseboats are a unique accommodation option in Kerala. You can book deluxe or luxury houseboats with meals and private backwater cruises.
Can I customize my hotel or resort stay in Kerala?
Yes, you can choose room categories, meal plans, and additional services like airport transfers, spa treatments, and sightseeing tours.
What is the best time to book hotels in Kerala?
The best time to book hotels in Kerala is from October to March. For monsoon discounts and Ayurveda treatments, June to September is ideal.
Do you offer airport pickup services?
Yes, we provide airport pickup and drop services at major airports including Kochi, Trivandrum, and Calicut at additional charges.
