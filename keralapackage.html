Kerala Tour FAQs

Everything you need to know about Kerala holiday packages

What is the best time to visit Kerala?
The best time to visit Kerala is from October to March when the weather is pleasant for hill stations, backwaters, and beaches.
Are Kerala honeymoon packages customizable?
Yes, Grand Green Holiday offers fully customizable Kerala honeymoon packages including Munnar, Alleppey houseboats, and Kovalam beach stays.
Do your Kerala tour packages include houseboat stay?
Yes, most of our Kerala tour packages include luxury or deluxe houseboat experiences in Alleppey with meals and scenic backwater cruises.
Is Kerala safe for family vacations?
Kerala is one of the safest travel destinations in India, ideal for family holidays, senior citizens, and group tours.
What destinations are covered in your Kerala tour packages?
Our Kerala packages cover popular destinations like Munnar, Alleppey, Thekkady, Kovalam, Wayanad, Kumarakom, and Varkala.
